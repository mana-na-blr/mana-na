Mood & Depression Awareness Check

Over the past two weeks, how often have you experienced the following?

1. I have felt sad, low, or emotionally drained.

2. I have lost interest or enjoyment in activities I usually like.

3. I have felt tired or lacked energy, even after resting.

4. I have found it difficult to concentrate, make decisions, or stay focused.

5. I have noticed changes in my sleep, such as sleeping too much or too little.

6. I have experienced changes in my appetite or weight without trying.

7. I have felt worthless, guilty, or overly critical of myself.

8. I have felt hopeless about the future.

9. I have found it difficult to carry out my daily responsibilities at home, school, or work.

10. I have had thoughts that life is not worth living or thoughts of harming myself.

Your response suggests you may be experiencing significant emotional distress.
We encourage you to reach out to a trusted family member, friend, or mental health professional as soon as possible.
