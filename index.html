Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What can I expect in the first session?

The first session is an initial consultation where we discuss your concerns, goals, and background. It's an opportunity for us to get to know each other and see if we're a good fit for working together.

How long are the sessions?

Standard counselling sessions typically last 50 to 60 minutes. The frequency of sessions is usually once a week, but this can be adjusted based on your specific needs.

Is therapy confidential?

Yes, confidentiality is a cornerstone of therapy. Everything discussed in our sessions remains private, with a few legal and ethical exceptions (such as immediate risk of harm to yourself or others).

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, we provide both in-person and online consultations via secure video platforms, allowing you to access support from the comfort of your own space.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment or by reaching out to us via the contact form on this website, emailing us directly, or sending a message on WhatsApp.
